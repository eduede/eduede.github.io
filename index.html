Eamon Duede

University of Chicago

I am a joint PhD student in the departments of Philosophy and the Committee on the Conceptual and Historical Studies of Science at the University of Chicago. Until recently, I was the Executive Director of KnowledgeLab (of which I am still a member). My work is broadly at the intersection of the philosophy of science and the computational social sciences. In the philosophy of science, I focus on formal aspects of artificial intelligence and machine learning in scientific knowledge. In the computational social sciences, I focus on the contribution of epistemic commitments to the dynamics of knowledge production.
